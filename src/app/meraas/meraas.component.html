About Meraas

Meraas is a Dubai-based developer redefining urban living through bold design, iconic locations, and lifestyle-focused communities. From beachside escapes to artistic city hubs, every Meraas development is a unique experience crafted for modern living. With destinations like Bluewaters Island, La Mer, and City Walk. Meraas blends architecture, culture, and leisure — turning every property into a story, and every neighborhood into a lifestyle. Meraas is not just building homes — it’s shaping Dubai’s most desirable places to live, connect, and thrive.
